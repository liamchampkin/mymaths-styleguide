We have released 16 new worksheets, which form a brand new KS1 booster pack. This new pack is closely aligned to the demands of the national curriculum at the end of KS1, and is a great way to embed topics learnt throughout the year.

These worksheets are a great resource to assign to your students ahead of their SATs. To set all of these tasks as a booster pack, simply select the booster pack from the drop-down in the task allocation window in the Assessment Manager, and select All worksheets.
